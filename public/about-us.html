How did founderpad start?

Our Story

The concept for founderpad was first thought up because of the struggle we have had in starting companies that didn't have a lot of potential, and hadn't been fully thought through or planned out. We found that it was very difficult to work out if a business idea was actually good or not, and realised that this was a large scale problem that countless others have faced in the past.

We wanted to create a platform where anybody with a business idea could share it with other like-minded individuals and get feedback and opinions on that idea. If an idea is upvoted and users are interested, that idea then has the potential to start and scale into a successful company. Ideas that don't receive many upvotes means that the idea maybe needs a little more thought, and this will help prevent ideas being started that have not been fully thought through or planned out.

We also wanted to create a space where individuals interested in joining a startup can collaborate with others, join startups and help create perfect founding teams for businesses that could become the next unicorns.
